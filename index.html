About me

I have completed my MSc study of Computational Statistics and Machine Learning at University College London (UCL), with the final performance of Distinction. The past years’ training has equipped me with a solid foundation in Machine Learning, and further stimulate my inner interest in the research of Natural Language Processing.

Like challenges and solving problems, and hope to achieve something significant; endlessly curious and seeking to extend my knowledge; can work with others in a team, but equally happy to concentrate on problems on my own.
